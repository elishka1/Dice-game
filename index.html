How to play

1. Click the 'roll' button to begin the game by rolling the dice.
2. The dice will roll a number between 1 and 6.
3. Each number that has been rolled will be added to your score.
4. If the dice rolls 1, then you're out and your score will be reset.
5. The first player to reach a score of 20 wins!
