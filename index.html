How to play

1. Each player has a 'roll' button to begin the game by clicking to roll the dice.
2. Take it in turns to roll the dice (once each turn).
3. The dice will roll a number between 1 and 6.
4. Each number that has been rolled will be added to your score.
5. If the dice rolls 1, then you're out!
6. The first player to reach a score of 20 wins!
